üìÖ Booking & Pricing

For best availability, we recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance. However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests with 48-72 hours notice, depending on our schedule. Popular dates (weekends, holidays) tend to book up faster, so earlier is always better!

Pricing is based on several factors:

  • Event duration - Typically 3-6 hour packages
  • Guest count - Affects preparation and service needs
  • Service level - Basic bar service vs. full cocktail experience
  • Custom requests - Themed cocktails, special setups, etc.
  • Location - Travel distance and setup requirements

Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific needs!

Yes, we require a 50% deposit to secure your booking date. The remaining balance is due on the day of service. We accept cash, check, Venmo, and most major credit cards.

We understand that plans can change:

  • 14+ days notice: Full refund of deposit
  • 7-13 days notice: 50% refund of deposit
  • Less than 7 days: Deposit is non-refundable

Weather-related cancellations for outdoor events are handled case-by-case.
