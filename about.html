About LeBlanc Solutions

LeBlanc Solutions is the creative vision of Jewel LeBlanc, offering bespoke and personalized bartending and event solutions with an artistic touch. Her mission is to blend artistry, hospitality, and innovation - crafting memorable experiences one event at a time.

Jewel's Story

What started as a passion for mixology and a penchant for dressing up has evolved into a full-service bartending experience that brings creativity to every event. Jewel combines her love for crafting unique cocktails with her natural ability to connect with people, creating an atmosphere where every guest feels welcomed and every drink tells a story.

From casual costume parties, to intimate get-togethers and elegant weddings, Jewel brings the same level of dedication and care to every event. Her approach isn't just about serving drinks - it's about creating moments, sparking conversations, and ensuring that your celebration is as unique as you are.
